Seasonal Favorites: What's New and What's Back

Panera Bread knows how to keep things fresh and exciting, and 2025 is no exception. This spring, the menu welcomes innovative creations and the return of beloved classics:

  • Croissant Toast Sandwiches: A first-of-its-kind, these sandwiches use croissant dough for a buttery, crispy bite. Choose from:

    • Croque Monsieur Croissant Toast: Black Forest ham, provolone, asiago, caramelized onion, and gruyere spread.

    • Fromage Croissant Toast: Provolone, American cheese, caramelized onions, arugula, and garlic aioli.

  • Strawberry Chicken Caprese Salad: A vibrant new salad with fresh strawberries, tender chicken, and classic Caprese flavors.

  • Strawberry Poppyseed Chicken Salad: A perennial favorite that's back for the season, combining juicy chicken, strawberries, and a tangy poppyseed dressing.

  • Mexican Street Corn Chowder: Creamy, hearty, and packed with sweet corn and bold spices-perfect for spring.

These limited-time offerings are available nationwide, so don't miss your chance to try them!

Customization Hacks: Make Your Meal Your Own

One of Panera's best features is the ability to customize your order. Here are some top hacks to get exactly what you crave:

  • Bagel Bread Swap: Upgrade your sandwich by swapping the standard bread for your favorite Panera bagel. This works especially well with the Pick Two menu.

  • DIY French Dip: Start with a baguette sandwich, swap chicken for steak, and ask for horseradish sauce instead of buffalo for a French dip twist.

  • Recreate Old Favorites: Miss the Sierra Turkey Sandwich? Order a Roasted Turkey sandwich, remove extras, add chipotle sauce and red onions, and swap the bread for an Asiago bagel.

  • Mac & Cheese Upgrade: Order a mac & cheese and a salad, then combine ingredients from both for a custom bowl-think salsa, cilantro, or avocado from the Baja Bowl.

  • Mix and Match Sides: Choose apple slices, chips, or a baguette as your side to balance out your meal.

Pro Tip: You can make up to five customizations per order, so get creative and build your perfect meal!
